The Faculty Feedback Analyzer is designed to help institutions and teachers gain meaningful insights from faculty feedback forms submitted by students. Teachers can upload their collected feedback data into the system to generate a detailed report that includes average ratings, sentiment analysis of student comments, and visual charts. These insights allow faculty members to understand how students perceive their teaching, recognize strengths, and identify areas where improvements can be made.
